The Yvonne Story

In just five months, Yvonne transformed her life from facing legal challenges and homelessness to securing two jobs, moving into her own apartment, and preparing to reunite with her daughter. Her journey shows the power of community support and access to the right resources. Thanks to Melanie at the Keys to Change Justice Opioid Program, the Homeless ID Project, and Tyler at St. Vincent de Paul's Workforce Opportunities Program, Yvonne has found stability, renewed purpose, and hope for the future.

Read more about Yvonne and her story at the Homeless ID Project.

The Berkeley Story

James and his three young children were sleeping on streets and in city parks, trying to avoid police and stay safe after losing their home. Unable to secure stable housing, James also struggled to enroll his children in school while navigating the challenges of homelessness. A dedicated volunteer named Barbara provided hot meals, compassion, and support, connecting the family with social workers and housing resources. Through her efforts and the assistance of local service providers, James and his children moved into supportive housing, allowing the children to return to school and giving the family a path toward long-term stability.

Read more about the Berkeley Family at Consider the Homeless)
