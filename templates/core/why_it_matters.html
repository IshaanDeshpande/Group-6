Why It Matters

Homelessness is still a major concern in Colorado

  • Colorado's 2024 Point in Time count showed a 30% increase in homelessness compared with the previous year, one of the largest increases in the nation. https://kdvr.com/news/local/report-homelessness-in-colorado-increased-30-in-2024/
  • More than 52,800 Coloradans were found to be using homelessness-related housing and support services in 2024, and updated data later increased that estimate to over 54,000 people. https://denverite.com/2026/05/19/colorado-homelessness-2025-statistics/

Volunteering directly supports programs proven to combat homelessness

  • Homelessness organizations in Colorado reported to have created housing opportunities for at least 27,448 people in just a single year https://www.mdhi.org/
