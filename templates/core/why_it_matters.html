Why It Matters

Homelessness is a local problem with human consequences.

ResourceConnect exists to make help easier to find and make community action easier to start.

People Need Fast Access

When someone is looking for shelter, food, health care, or basic services, clear information can reduce the time between need and support.

Communities Need Direction

Many people want to help but do not know where to begin. Education and guided opportunities turn concern into practical action.

Connection Builds Momentum

Bringing resources, learning, and volunteer pathways together helps residents and organizations work from the same place.
