About Us

Built for Computing 4 Good 2026.

ResourceConnect is a Colorado School of Mines student project focused on helping people experiencing homelessness find resources and helping community members get involved.

Our Focus

We are organizing the site around two main branches: one for finding resources and one for learning how to help.

What Comes Next

The project is still in development, with planned features such as searchable resources, guided involvement options, educational pages, and lightweight interactive tools.
